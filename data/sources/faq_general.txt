Q: What is a .my domain?
A: The .my domain is Malaysia's country-code top-level domain (ccTLD) managed by MyNIC.

Q: Who can register a .my domain?
A: Any individual or organisation with a presence in Malaysia can register a .my domain name.

Q: How do I renew my domain?
A: Log in to SELFCARE or contact your registrar before the expiry date to renew your domain name.

Q: Who can register a .edu.my domain?
A: Educational institutions recognised by the Ministry of Education or Ministry of Higher Education are eligible for .edu.my.

Q: What happens if my domain expires?
A: After the expiry date, the domain enters a 45-day renewal grace period. If not renewed, it will be deleted from the registry.
